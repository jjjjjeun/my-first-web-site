Work

Corporation is responsible for the following tasks ( Article 14, Paragraph 1 of the National Health Insurance Act ). Qualification management of national health insurance subscribers and dependents Imposition and collection of national health insurance premiums and other levies Management of National Health Insurance Benefits Preventive projects necessary to maintain and improve the health of National Health Insurance subscribers and their dependents Payment of National Health Insurance Benefit Costs Other tasks entrusted under the National Health Insurance Act or other laws and regulations Other work deemed necessary by the Minister of Health and Welfare in relation to health insurance.
